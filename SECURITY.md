# Security Policy

[English](SECURITY.md) | [简体中文](SECURITY.zh-CN.md)

Please do not open a public issue for a suspected vulnerability. Email `tonyhyworld@gmail.com` with the affected version or commit, component, reproduction steps, impact, and suggested mitigation. Do not include API keys, customer data, or destructive payloads.

High-priority reports include sandbox escape, unauthorized tool or MCP access, credential exposure, cross-user data access, authentication or permission bypass, unsafe scenario extraction, forged observations/events/settlements, and replay injection affecting viewers.

We aim to acknowledge reports within 3 business days and provide an initial triage within 7 business days. These are targets, not contractual service levels. Good-faith research that avoids privacy violations, service disruption, data destruction, and unauthorized access is welcome.
