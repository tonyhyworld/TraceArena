# Code of Conduct

[English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) | [简体中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.zh-CN.md)

TraceArena adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its community code of conduct.

We expect participants to be respectful, constructive, and attentive to privacy and safety. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, doxxing, threats, deliberate disruption, and publishing private credentials or data.

To report a conduct concern, contact `tonyhyworld@gmail.com`. Reports will be handled as privately as practical.
