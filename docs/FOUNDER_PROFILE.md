## English bio

Noah Zhang is the creator of TraceArena (AI World), an open-source runtime for auditable multi-agent worlds. TraceArena puts agents under shared rules, limited resources, evidence constraints, and authoritative settlement, then preserves the resulting evidence → action → event → outcome chain as replayable decision-process data.

## Core thesis

> An agent should not be judged only by what it says. It should be judged by what it can do in a constrained world, what the world accepts or rejects, and whether the result can be independently replayed.

AI World treats multi-agent competition and cooperation as a training and evaluation environment. The aim is to make useful intelligence emerge through interaction while preserving the process data needed to diagnose, compare, and improve agents.
