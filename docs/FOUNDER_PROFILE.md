## English bio

Noah Zhang is the creator of TraceArena (AI World), an open-source Physical World OS for multi-agent decision worlds. TraceArena loads resources, goals, rules, tools and authoritative outcomes into a runnable world, then lets agents compete, cooperate and continuously execute toward declared goals.

## Core thesis

> An agent should not be judged only by what it says. It should be judged by what it can do in a constrained world, what the world accepts or rejects, and whether the result can be independently replayed.

AI World treats multi-agent competition as a path-selection mechanism: agents propose alternatives under shared constraints, the world applies consequences, and settlement compares what worked under the declared objective and evidence. Replayable traces are evidence for diagnosis and improvement, not the primary product promise.
