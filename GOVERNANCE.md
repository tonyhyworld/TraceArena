# TraceArena Governance

[English](GOVERNANCE.md) | [简体中文](GOVERNANCE.zh-CN.md)

TraceArena begins with lightweight founder-led governance. The Project Lead is 张诺亚.

Routine bug fixes, tests, documentation, examples, and compatible improvements are reviewed through pull requests. Changes to public contracts, settlement authority, security boundaries, licensing, or governance should first be discussed in a design proposal.

Maintainers protect the generic-runtime/scenario boundary, require evidence for behavioral claims, and review contributions respectfully. Commercial users do not receive authority to alter objective results, bypass security review, or claim official certification.

The Apache-2.0 license does not grant permission to use TraceArena trademarks, official certification marks, or imply endorsement. Community projects may say they are “built with TraceArena”; only authorized projects may use “Official” or “TraceArena Certified.”
